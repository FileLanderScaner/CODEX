# AhorroYA Tester Consent And Disclosure

## Plain-Language Disclosure

You are testing a staging/preproduction version of AhorroYA.

The goal is to learn whether users understand the app, can compare prices, see estimated savings, and would share or use it again.

## Important Limitations

- This is not public production.
- The app may have bugs.
- Some data may be incomplete or outdated.
- Savings are estimates, not guarantees.
- PayPal live payments are not active.
- Do not enter unnecessary sensitive information.

## Feedback Use

Your feedback may be summarized to improve:

- Search.
- Price comparison.
- Savings explanation.
- Trust and clarity.
- WhatsApp sharing.
- Premium positioning.

Do not include private credentials, payment data, passwords, or sensitive personal details in feedback.

## Consent Statement

By testing AhorroYA in this controlled group, you agree that:

- You understand this is staging/preproduction.
- You understand no real payment should be made.
- You can stop testing at any time.
- Your feedback may be used to improve the product.
