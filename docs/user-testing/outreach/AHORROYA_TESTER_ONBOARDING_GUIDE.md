# AhorroYA Tester Onboarding Guide

## What Testers Need To Know

AhorroYA helps compare supermarket prices and find estimated savings.

This test is:

- Controlled.
- Staging/preproduction.
- Protected.
- Not public production.
- Not connected to PayPal live.

## What Testers Should Do

1. Open the staging link provided by the operator.
2. Read the first screen.
3. Search for a product they know.
4. Review the comparison.
5. Identify the cheapest option.
6. Check the estimated saving.
7. Try WhatsApp share or copy.
8. Look at Premium and say whether it is valuable.
9. Send feedback using the questions provided.

## What Testers Should Not Do

- Do not enter unnecessary sensitive data.
- Do not attempt real payments.
- Do not treat prices as guaranteed.
- Do not post the protected staging link publicly.
- Do not share any token, password, or private link if one is provided separately.

## Expected Time

5 to 10 minutes for the first test.

## Feedback Needed

- What was clear?
- What was confusing?
- Did the savings feel useful?
- Did the app feel trustworthy?
- Would you use it again?
- Would you share it?
- What should be fixed before inviting more users?
