# AhorroYA First 10 Users Pilot Plan

## Goal

Validate the core experience with 10 trusted users before expanding to 25, 50, and 100.

## Pilot Profile

| User type | Count | Reason |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Household grocery buyer | 4 | Core buyer behavior |
| Student or fixed-budget worker | 2 | Savings sensitivity |
| Parent/family buyer | 2 | Repeat supermarket purchases |
| Small commerce/contact | 1 | Trust and market perspective |
| Product/tech reviewer | 1 | Fast bug detection |

## Required Task

Each user must:

1. Open staging.
2. Explain what AhorroYA does.
3. Search one product.
4. Identify cheapest result.
5. Explain estimated saving.
6. Try WhatsApp share or copy.
7. Review Premium CTA.
8. Give feedback.

## Expected Metrics

| Metric | Pass Criteria For First 10 |
| --- | --- |
| 5-second value understanding | At least 7 of 10 |
| Search completed | At least 7 of 10 |
| Cheapest result understood | At least 6 of 10 |
| Share/copy attempted | At least 3 of 10 |
| Would use again | At least 4 of 10 |
| Critical bugs | 0 |

## Final Question

"What is the one thing we must fix before showing this to more people?"

## Escalation To Next 25 Users

Move to 25 users only if:

- No CRITICAL issues.
- No payment/security confusion that creates risk.
- Search completion is at least 70%.
- Users can explain the app without coaching.
- Top HIGH issues have owner and fix plan.

If criteria are not met, run a focused Codex cycle:

- `UX_REFINEMENT` for search/confusion.
- `TRUST_AND_COPY_POLISH` for price confidence.
- `GROWTH_LOOP_POLISH` for sharing friction.
- `MOBILE_POLISH` for device issues.
