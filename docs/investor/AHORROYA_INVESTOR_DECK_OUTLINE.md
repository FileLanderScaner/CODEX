# AhorroYA Investor Deck Outline

Date: 2026-05-11

## Slide 1: Title

- AhorroYA
- "Comparar precios y encontrar oportunidades de ahorro en segundos"
- Uruguay-first, LATAM-ready thesis

## Slide 2: Problem

- Grocery price comparison is fragmented.
- Consumers waste time and miss savings.
- WhatsApp groups and manual browsing are inefficient.

## Slide 3: Solution

- Search product.
- Compare available prices.
- See estimated savings.
- Share by WhatsApp.
- Save favorites/alerts.

## Slide 4: Product Demo

- Staging demo only.
- Show search, cheapest price, savings, WhatsApp share, Premium CTA.
- Label as preproduction/staging.

## Slide 5: Market

- Uruguay initial wedge.
- LATAM expansion potential.
- Grocery/household repeated-purchase behavior.

## Slide 6: Business Model

- Premium subscriptions.
- Affiliates/referrals.
- Ads/sponsored placements with labeling.
- B2B data insights.
- Retail/brand partnerships.

## Slide 7: Technology

- Expo/React Native Web.
- Node API.
- Supabase/RLS.
- PayPal sandbox.
- Vercel Preview/Staging.
- AI agents disabled and gated.

## Slide 8: Traction / Current State

- Use only evidenced claims:
  - staging ready for first 100 users;
  - tests/build/checks pass;
  - RLS pass;
  - PayPal sandbox ready.
- Do not claim revenue, production launch, or live payments.

## Slide 9: Competition

- Manual supermarket browsing.
- Retailer apps.
- Coupon/discount pages.
- Generic marketplaces.

Differentiation:

- Savings-first UX.
- WhatsApp-native loop.
- Uruguay-local starting point.

## Slide 10: Roadmap

- Close production blockers.
- First 100 users.
- Data coverage expansion.
- Premium validation.
- Partnerships and B2B insights.

## Slide 11: Investment / Use Of Funds

Possible use:

- Data coverage and ingestion.
- Production security/compliance.
- Growth experiments.
- Partnerships.
- Product/engineering.

Do not ask for funds based on unverified traction.

## Slide 12: Risks And Mitigations

- Data freshness: source labeling and reports.
- Payment live: blocked until credentials/test complete.
- Production readiness: release gate.
- Trust: cautious claims and "ahorro estimado".

## Closing

- Ask for strategic feedback, pilot partners, or pre-seed conversations depending on audience.
