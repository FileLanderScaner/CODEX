# AhorroYA First 100 Users Plan

Date: 2026-05-11

## Target User

Initial users:

- Montevideo shoppers comparing supermarket prices.
- Parents buying repeated household products.
- Students and workers optimizing weekly groceries.
- People active in WhatsApp neighborhood or family groups.

## Value Proposition

```text
AhorroYA te muestra donde comprar mas barato en segundos.
```

Safe supporting copy:

- Compara precios disponibles.
- Encontra oportunidades de ahorro.
- Ahorro estimado.
- Precios sujetos a disponibilidad.
- Verifica siempre antes de comprar.

## Acquisition Channels

| Channel | First message | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| WhatsApp family/friends | "Estoy probando AhorroYA para comparar precios en Montevideo. Busca yerba, leche o arroz y decime si te sirve." | First testers |
| Neighborhood groups | "Arme una app para encontrar diferencias de precio entre supers. Si compras en Disco, Devoto, Ta-Ta o Tienda Inglesa, probala." | Local relevance |
| Instagram Reels | Show one product with two prices and savings | Awareness |
| TikTok short | "No compres antes de comparar este precio" | Viral curiosity |
| Facebook Uruguay groups | Practical savings post, no exaggerated claims | Trust |
| Small stores | Ask for product price contributions | Supply side |

## 7-Day Plan

| Day | Action | Success signal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Share with 15 close contacts | 10 opens, 5 searches |
| 2 | Post 3 real product comparisons | 20 opens, 10 searches |
| 3 | Ask users to share one savings result | 5 WhatsApp shares |
| 4 | Collect top missing products | 10 missing-product replies |
| 5 | Push Premium concept survey | 5 responses |
| 6 | Post improved examples from feedback | Search completion improves |
| 7 | Review metrics and top blockers | Clear next iteration |

## 30-Day Plan

1. Reach 100 users through personal WhatsApp, groups, and short videos.
2. Measure search-to-result conversion.
3. Add top 20 repeated products.
4. Improve zero-results states.
5. Validate whether users understand Premium.
6. Run PayPal sandbox end-to-end before live.
7. Keep production blocked until real external gates pass.

## Success Criteria

- 100 real users open the app.
- 50 users perform at least one search.
- 25 users see a cheapest price.
- 10 users share or copy a savings result.
- 5 users click Premium.
- 0 production secrets exposed.
- 0 production deploys without gates.

## Failure Signals

- Users do not understand what to search.
- Users do not trust the price source.
- No one shares savings.
- Most searches return empty.
- Premium CTA is ignored.
- PayPal sandbox fails signature/webhook validation.

## Iteration Loop

Every 48 hours:

1. Review searches and zero-results.
2. Add or improve product examples.
3. Improve share copy.
4. Fix confusing UI text.
5. Re-run checks.
